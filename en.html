About Sheikh Khaled Alqassar

Sheikh Khaled Alqassar is an imam and mosque speaker with more than a decade of experience in delivering khutbahs, teaching, and community engagement. His work focuses on:

  • Balancing knowledge with wisdom and mercy in da‘wah
  • Addressing practical issues faced by families and youth
  • Connecting the Qur'an and Sunnah to modern challenges
